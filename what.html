Why What Works

If you want what we want, feel free to create anything under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license attributed to Agitproper.

When you create something for Agitproper, simply ask: will this help whoever sees or hears this win in a not-safe world one day? If the answer is 'Yes', it's perfect for Agitproper. Imagine a disaster. A tsunami. A hurricane. A nuclear holocaust. Someone getting thrown out of a community. Now imagine what it would be like to have people who are competent responding and creating in the face of this disaster. Imagine their faces- some serene, some grimaced in the extreme, some grinning in pain, but also full of wonder with hints of joy. The joy of focusing, of getting lost in the task at hand and creating life where all hope was lost- whether that's to pull someone out of a burning building or to offer a hand in support after a long day of demeaning work. We want what we make to help people feel this feeling, whenever they need it. To overcome, whenever they need it. To expand, take risks, and explore, whenever they need it. To flourish, whenever we all need to flourish.

Contact us through agency[at]foretrek.com to join our chat and receive whatever files you may need from us.

None of us can do this alone- changing the kind of stories we tell is something that could take a lifetime, but it's very possible with your help.

All we're going to need is 25 projects (and we're at 3) - that's enough to show people what our world could look like-

that's enough for your neighbors to spark a conversation,

that's enough to convince your friends to make more projects that celebrate yourselves,

that's enough to start a New World.
